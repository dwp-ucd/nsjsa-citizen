What happens next

Wait for us to contact you to discuss your next steps towards finding work.

If you are able to look for work safely while following government guidance on coronavirus (COVID-19) you should: 

  • update your CV
  • consider your opportunities for returning to work
  • search for jobs, for example on the government's Job Help website
  • make yourself available for work

You only need to contact us to report a change of circumstances, such as starting a new job.
