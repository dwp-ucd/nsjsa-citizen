Apply for new style Jobseeker’s Allowance

Jobseeker’s Allowance is a benefit for people of working age who are out of work or work less than 16 hours a week on average. It’s paid while you’re looking for work.

You cannot get new style Jobseeker’s Allowance if you’ve never worked.

Check if you are eligible

You must:

  • live in England, Scotland or Wales
  • be looking for work
  • have paid enough National Insurance contributions over the last 2 to 3 years
  • be over 18 and under State Pension age

Before you apply

You will need:

  • your National Insurance number
  • your bank or building society details
  • details of your recent employment
  • details of pensions you receive

After you apply

  • we’ll text or call you within 48 hours, with your Jobcentre Plus interview details
