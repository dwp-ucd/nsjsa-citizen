Armed forces Travelling abroad with the armed forces

You may be able to get National Insurance credits if you are the spouse or registered civil partner of a member of the armed forces and you went with them on an overseas posting in the last 4 years.

Credits can help to fill gaps in your National Insurance record, for example if you were not in work when you were with your partner on an overseas posting. You can tell us here so that we can check when we assess your claim.
