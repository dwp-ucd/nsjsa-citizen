Enable a change to claim start date (by citizen only) 1420: Iteration 1 (10th August 2020)

Change claim start date affects a number of sections that may, or maynot, have had data input by the citizen during the course of the information gather.

In particular, these sections are:

  • Jury service
    Citizens will be asked to confirm if they've been on jury service in the last 12 weeks against the new start date
  • Previous employment ended within the last 6 months
    In such instances previous employment entries will be removed if beyond the 6 month window, citizens will be asked if they've further previous employment they wish to enter as part of their application against the new start date
  • Worked or claimed benefits outside of the United Kingdom
    Citizens will be asked to confirm if they've worked or claimed benefits outside of the United Kingdom in the last 4 years against the new start date (suspected edge case)
  • Current pension that is due to increase within 6 months
    Any current pensions already entered and acknowledged as increasing in the next 6 months will be amended accordingly in the event the new start date invalidates this
  • Future pensions within 6 months
    In such instances future pension entries will be removed if beyond the 6 month window, citizens will be asked if they've further future pensions they wish to enter as part of their application against the new start date
  • Education and training
    Citizens will be asked to confirm if they've undertaken any education or training in the last 4 years against the new start date (suspected edge case)

From check your answers:

  1. from "Check your answers"
  2. citizens "Change" the start date
  3. citizens are asked to confirm their action
  4. citizens are then asked to enter the new start date
  5. citizens are then notified as to which sections detailed above are affected
  6. citizens then confirm if they wish to proceed, whereby they will enter new/amend the information previously gathered

Please note: this does not include the new eligibilty question set.
