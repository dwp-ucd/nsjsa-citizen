Application complete

If you’ve provided a mobile number or email address, we’ll send you confirmation of your application and keep you updated on its progress.

What happens next

We’ll process your application.

If you’re eligible

If you can get New Style JSA, we’ll contact you about an appointment to agree what you need to do to look for work. This agreement is called a ‘Claimant Commitment’. We’ll usually contact you about the appointment within 10 days.

The appointment will be with a work coach and will normally be over the phone.

You must attend the appointment and agree your Claimant Commitment before we can pay you. Once you’ve agreed your Claimant Commitment and if you’re due a payment, we’ll aim to pay you within 7 days.

If you’re not eligible

If you cannot get New Style JSA, we’ll send you a letter to explain why. We’ll usually send the letter within 10 days.

Backdating your claim

In certain circumstances, you can start your claim from an earlier date than today. If your claim is eligible for backdating, you can still only get New Style JSA for up to 182 days (about 6 months) from your claim start date.

If you're eligible for New Style JSA

You can discuss backdating with your work coach at your first appointment. They will advise you if you can backdate your claim or not.

If you're not eligible for New Style JSA

Once your letter arrives, contact Jobcentre Plus if you still want to apply for backdating.

If you want to ask for backdating today

There are 3 ways to get the JSA5 form, which you must complete and post back to us so that we can consider your claim for backdating.

To get the JSA5 form, you can either:

  • download the JSA5.pdf form (23Kb) and print at home or somewhere convenient
  • collect the JSA5 form from your local Jobcentre Plus office before or during your first appointment with a work coach
  • call the Benefit Enquiry Line and ask for the JSA5 form to be sent you by post Telephone: 0800 055 6688 Textphone: 0800 023 4888

Once you’ve completed the JSA5 form, you must send it to the following address:
