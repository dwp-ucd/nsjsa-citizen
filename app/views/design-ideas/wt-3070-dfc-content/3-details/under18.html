You cannot apply online for New Style Jobseeker's Allowance

Based on your date of birth, you cannot apply online for New Style Jobseeker’s Allowance. To apply online you must be 18 or over.

If you think you are eligible, you can apply by contacting Jobcentre Plus if you live in England, Wales or Scotland, or the Jobs and Benefits office if you live in Northern Ireland.

If you live in England, Wales or Scotland

To apply for New Style Jobseeker’s Allowance contact Jobcentre Plus.
You can also find your nearest Jobcentre Plus.

If you live in Northern Ireland

To apply for New Style Jobseeker’s Allowance contact or visit your nearest Jobs and Benefits office.
