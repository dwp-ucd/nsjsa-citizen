Jira reference: 1420 Change claim start date

An effort to allow people to change their claim start date at the end of their application without losing all of the information from their application.

Currently, if we were to enable this functionality, and the customer were to proceed with a date change, the information gathered would be cleared from the cache, but without the customer being aware of this – thereby submitting an incomplete application.

Worked on by: Neil Holroyd, Gareth Joyce, Poppy Davis

Versions

  • 30 November 2020

    #7 Changing the questions to negate the need to answer them again

    Note: This prototype is based on the assumption that time frames we ask about in the data gathering are not required to change because of the Linking Periods rule, other than two questions where the dates need to be extended.

    Note: the second version of this (not prototyped) relates to a customer backdating their claim in the first instance and deciding later they should change it to today. From the Summary they would change the date and then be taken directly back to the Summary page.
