How to find out about your National Insurance contributions

You need enough Class 1 National Insurance contributions in the last 2 tax years to get a payment from New Style JSA.

You will have got them either through paid employment, getting a benefit that pays them for you, or a combination. National Insurance credits can also count.

Contact HM Revenue & Customs (HMRC)

You can call HMRC for help and information about Class 1 National Insurance.
