Pension or annuity Are you getting money from a pension or an annuity?

You can tell us about more than one and you can enter them in any order.

A pension is an income you receive after you retire from a job. An annuity is an insurance product that guarantees a regular income for the rest of your life.

You do not need to tell us about a pension or annuity if it's inherited from someone who died.
