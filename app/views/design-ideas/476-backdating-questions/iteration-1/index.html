Revised backdating questions 476: Iteration 1 (4th August 2020)

With regards to Check your answers, it has been noted that:

  • 4x current employer details can be entered
  • 4x previous employer details can be entered
  • 9x pensions can be entered; this being a combination of current, deferred, and future pensions (not 9 of each)

It is worth nothing, that in the event the maximum number of pensions have been entered (for example, 9x current pensions) then the subsequent removal of one or more current pensions does not open up the opportunity to then add deferred or future pensions.
