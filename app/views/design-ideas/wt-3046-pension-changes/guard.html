Pension or annuity Are you getting money from a pension or an annuity?

If you have more than one pension or annuity you will be able to tell us about the others later. You can enter them in any order.

A pension represents the savings you draw on after you retire from a job, or reach retirement age.

An annuity is an insurance product that allows you to swap your pension savings for a guaranteed regular income that will last for the rest of your life.

You do not need to tell us about a pension or annuity if it's inherited from someone who died.
